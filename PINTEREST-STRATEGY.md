# SkynetLabs Pinterest Strategy — Batch 5 Carousels

## Why Pinterest is Different

Pinterest is NOT social media. It's a **visual search engine**. People come to Pinterest to:
- Search for solutions, tools, templates, and guides
- Save content for later (pins have 3-6 month shelf life vs 48 hours on IG/LinkedIn)
- Click through to websites (Pinterest has the highest referral traffic of any social platform)
- Discover — the algorithm surfaces content to people who never followed you

**Key insight:** A pin posted today can drive traffic 6 months from now. LinkedIn posts die in 48 hours.

## Traffic Funnel Strategy

```
Pinterest Pin (tall infographic, keyword-rich)
    ↓ click
skynetjoe.com/?utm_source=pinterest&utm_medium=pin&utm_campaign=batch5-carousels
    ↓ browse
Service pages / Portfolio / Contact
    ↓ convert
Discovery call booking / Email signup / Tool usage
```

### UTM Tracking
Every pin links to `skynetjoe.com` with UTM parameters:
- `utm_source=pinterest` — identifies Pinterest as traffic source
- `utm_medium=pin` — identifies the medium
- `utm_campaign=batch5-carousels` — identifies this batch
- `utm_content=pin_XX` — identifies specific pin

**Track in Google Analytics:** Acquisition → Traffic Acquisition → filter by utm_source=pinterest

## Pinterest SEO Best Practices Applied

### 1. Title Optimization (100 chars max)
- Front-loaded with search keywords
- Include numbers (10, 7, 15 — numbers get 2x more saves)
- Include year (2026) for freshness signals
- Include format type (Guide, Checklist, Framework, Comparison)
- NO hashtags in titles

### 2. Description Optimization (500 chars max)
- First 50 chars are the most important (shown in search preview)
- Natural keyword integration (not keyword stuffing)
- Include the value proposition ("save $9K/month", "5-step framework")
- Soft CTA at the end ("at skynetjoe.com")
- NO hashtags — Pinterest deprecated hashtag search in 2024

### 3. Image Optimization
- Tall pins (1:1.94 ratio) get 60% more engagement than square
- Text overlay on pins (title + stat + summary = information-dense)
- Brand watermark visible but not dominant
- 6 visual design families = variety in feed (prevents fatigue)

### 4. Posting Schedule
- **Time:** 2 PM EST (afternoon Pinterest browsing peak)
- **Frequency:** 1 pin/weekday (Mon-Fri)
- **Duration:** 6 weeks (Apr 13 → May 22)
- **Spacing:** Same time daily for algorithm consistency

## Pinterest-Specific Content Strategy

### What Goes Viral on Pinterest (tech/business niche):
1. **Checklists & Cheat Sheets** — "n8n Cheat Sheet", "Agency Setup Checklist"
2. **Comparison Charts** — "ChatGPT vs Claude vs Gemini"
3. **Number-Led Lists** — "10 Pricing Models", "15 Free Tools", "7 Books"
4. **How-To Guides** — "How to Audit Any Business for Automation"
5. **Templates & Frameworks** — "Agency OS", "Weekly Rhythm", "Saying No Scripts"
6. **Data/Stats Pins** — "$9.3K savings", "90% will die", "94% accuracy"

### What DOESN'T Work on Pinterest:
- Engagement bait ("drop a 👇", "DM me", polls)
- Short-lived opinions or hot takes
- Behind-the-scenes personal content
- Hashtag-heavy captions
- Dark, text-heavy images without visual hierarchy

## GHL Upload Instructions

### Step 1: Cancel/Delete Old Pinterest Posts
1. Open GHL → Marketing → Social Planner
2. Filter by Pinterest platform
3. Delete all FUTURE (unposted) Pinterest posts from the old IG+Pinterest CSV
4. Leave Instagram posts untouched

### Step 2: Upload New Pinterest CSV
1. GHL → Marketing → Social Planner → Bulk Upload → Advanced
2. Upload: `output/ghl-pinterest-seo.csv`
3. Select **ONLY Pinterest** as the platform
4. Verify the schedule (Apr 13 → May 22, weekdays, 2 PM EST)
5. Hit Publish

### Step 3: Verify
1. Check first pin posts correctly (Apr 13)
2. Verify tall pin image displays properly
3. Confirm UTM link works and tracks in GA4
4. Check Pinterest board assignment
